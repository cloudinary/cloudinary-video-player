Cloudinary Video Player

Autoplay on scroll

Video will start playing once the majority of the player is visible in the viewport, and pause when the majority is out of the viewport.
Furthermore, interacting with the player in any other way will stop the above behaviour: (e.g. pausing the video using the 'pause' button and scrolling up and then down will not resume the playing video again).

Scroll down to see the player and then up to see the above in action.

Example Code:

      HTML tag attributes:

      <video
        id="example-player"
        controls
        muted
        class="cld-video-player cld-video-player-skin-dark"
        data-cld-public-id="oceans"
        data-cld-autoplay-mode='on-scroll'>
      </video>


      JavaScript:

      var cld = cloudinary.Cloudinary.new({ cloud_name: 'miki-cloudinary' });

      // Initialize player
      var players = cld.videoPlayer('example-player');
